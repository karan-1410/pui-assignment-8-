OVERVIEW
Cybernetics as an Alternative to Big Data AI
I worked under Professor Paul Pangaro of the Human-Computer Interaction Institute to understand the nature of “Today’s AI” and the negative effects that it has on human-machine interaction. Professor Pangaro introduced me to the idea of cybernetic systems as an alternative to the current “Big-Data” AI systems that are mechanistic and non-personalised.

I created a concept map to explain the impact of using cybernetics and conversation as the foundation to designing machines and the issues that ‘Big Data AI’ constitutes. A concept map would help me create a systematic representation that would communicate how cybernetic systems work in contrast to ‘Today’s AI’ in order to incorporate individuality in Human-machine Interactions.
