OVERVIEW
Blockchain Technology: Empowering Refugees
This project was a Co-study with a group of 15 students. I am researching the topics of digital governance, technocracy and the evolving systems of blockchains because I want to find out if technology can actually create a sustainable solution in terms of reaching migration equity.

I was specifically intrigued by blockchain technology because of its abitlity to be transmissible, immutable and peer-to-peer to help cross borders with the use digital identities and ability to send remitances without fees.
